## English

Natalie lived in New York City. It was too hot. She felt very uncomfortable. She fell sick. She wanted to relax. She could not bear the heat. It was not her cup of tea. She was bored. She wanted a change. She decided to go to her aunt’s place. Her aunt lived in Boston. Natalie packed her bags. She left for Boston. She was very happy. She reached the destination. She was shocked. Boston was hotter than New York. She was disheartened. Her aunt picked her up. Natalie was not happy. She told her aunt she had come from hot to hot. Her aunt took her to an ice-cream parlor. Natalie had her favorite ice cream. She felt better.
