## English

Andrew had a pet dog. He called his dog Buddy. One fine morning, Buddy gave birth to eight babies. The babies were very adorable. They did not even open their eyes. Andrew was very happy. He took proper care of the babies. He fed them properly. The babies started to grow up. They were now able to move. Andrew took them to the veterinary doctor for regular checkups. Andrew loved playing with his dogs. His parents built a dog house. All the babies and the mother dog lived comfortably in the doghouse. Andrew brought food for his dogs. He took his dogs for a walk in the park every morning. Andrew played with his dogs.

## Português

Andrew tinha uma cadela de estimação. Ele chamava sua cadela de Buddy. Uma boa manhã, Buddy deu à luz oito bebês. Os bebês eram muito adoráveis. Eles nem sequer abriram os olhos. Andrew estava muito feliz. Ele cuidava bem dos bebês. Ele alimentou-os adequadamente. Os bebês começaram a crescer. Agora podiam se mover. Andrew levou-os ao médico veterinário para exames regulares. Andrew amava brincar com seus cachorros. Seus pais construíram uma casa de cachorros. Todos os bebês e a cadela viviam confortavelmente na casinha. Andrew trazia comida para seus cachorros. Ele levava seus cães para uma caminhada no Park todas as manhãs. Andrew brincava com seus cães.
