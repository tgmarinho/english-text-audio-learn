## English

It was a Sunday. Annabelle had just finished with her exams. She had been waiting for this day a long time. She was very happy. She was going on a trip. Annabelle and her friends left for a local beach. It was a fun time for them. Annabelle’s father went along with them. They reached it on time. All of them jumped into the water in their beachwear. They had fun at the beach. They clicked pictures. They danced. They sang. They were hungry. Annabelle’s father bought them some snacks. They were full. They wanted to relax. They took a sun bath. It was time to leave. It grew darker in the evening. They came back home with lots of beautiful memories. Annabelle said thank you to her father and went to sleep.

## Português

Era um domingo. Annabelle acabara de terminar os exames. Ela estava esperando por esse dia há muito tempo. Ela estava muito feliz. Ela estava indo para uma viagem. Annabelle e seus amigos partiram para uma praia local. Foi um momento divertido com eles. O pai de Annabelle os acompanhou. Eles chegaram lá no horário. Todos eles saltaram na água em sua roupa de praia. Eles se divertiram na praia. Eles tiraram fotos. Eles dançaram. Eles cantaram. Eles estavam com fome. O pai de Annabelle comprou alguns petiscos para eles. Eles estavam cheios. Eles queriam relaxar. Eles tomaram um banho de sol. Era hora de partir. A noite ficou mais escura. Eles voltaram para casa com muitas lembranças bonitas. Annabelle disse obrigado a seu pai e foi dormir.
