## English

John was a football player. He was on the school’s football team. He was very talented. One day while playing football he hit his legs. It hurt him a lot. He was not able to move. He had to see a doctor. The doctor asked him to get some rest. It was the final match of the tournament. John wanted to play the match. His mother asked him not to play. But he did not listen to her. The next day he again went to the field. John felt extreme pain while running. He was not able to move. The referee sent him out of the field. He told his mother that his leg hurts while he runs. John went back home and rested.

## Português

John era um jogador de futebol. Ele estava na equipe de futebol da escola. Ele era muito talentoso. Um dia, enquanto jogava futebol ele chocou suas pernas. Doeu muito. Ele não era capaz de se mover. Ele tinha que ver um médico. O médico pediu-lhe para descansar um pouco. Era a última partida do torneio. John queria jogar o jogo. Sua mãe pediu-lhe para não jogar. Mas ele não a ouvia. No dia seguinte voltou ao campo. John sentia dor extrema enquanto corria. Ele não era capaz de se mover. O árbitro mandou ele sair do campo. Ele disse a sua mãe que suas pernas doem enquanto ele corre. John voltou para casa e descansou.
