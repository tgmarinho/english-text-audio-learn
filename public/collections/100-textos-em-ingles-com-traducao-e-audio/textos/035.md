## English

David was a bad boy. He never got up early. He was always late for school. He missed his bus daily. He never listened to his parents. His teachers always complained about him. He never completed his homework. He troubled his fellow classmates. He left all his belongings at school. His parents were very upset. David was very rude to his elders. David’s parents decided to send him to a boarding school. He became very upset. He cried a lot. He did not want to go to the boarding school. He was very well aware of the strict rules. He was very sorry. He promised his parents that he would not repeat his mistakes again. Now, David was a good boy.

## Português

David era um menino mau. Ele nunca levantava cedo. Ele estava sempre atrasado para a escola. Ele perdia o ônibus diariamente. Ele nunca ouvia seus pais. Seus professores sempre reclamavam dele. Ele nunca completava sua lição de casa. Ele incomodava seus companheiros de classe. Ele deixava todos os seus pertences na escola. Seus pais estavam muito chateados. David era muito rude com os mais velhos. Os pais de David decidiram mandá-lo para um internato. Ele ficou muito chateado. Ele chorava muito. Ele não queria ir para o internato. Ele estava muito bem ciente das regras estritas. Ele estava muito triste. Ele prometeu a seus pais que não repetiria seus erros novamente. Agora, David era um bom menino.
