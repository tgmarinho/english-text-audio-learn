## English

Daniel and his friends decided to go to the park in the evening. They were busy playing. Suddenly they noticed a bolt out of the blue. They saw a flying saucer landing in front of them. They looked astonished. The door of the flying saucer opened automatically. Two aliens came out of it. They were not too tall; they had big eyes and big heads. However, they had small bodies. Daniel gathered courage. He went towards them to ask why they have come to Earth. One of them said that he has come to collect some samples from Earth. They moved around in the park for some time. They picked up some stones, flowers, and rocks. It was time for them to leave. Daniel was completely shocked. The aliens walked into the flying saucer and flew away.

## Português

Daniel e seus amigos decidiram ir ao parque à noite. Eles estavam ocupados brincando. De repente, eles notaram um raio no céu. Eles viram um disco voador aterrissando na frente deles. Eles ficaram surpresos. A porta do disco voador se abriu automaticamente. Dois extraterrestres saíram dele. Eles não eram muito altos; Eles tinham olhos grandes e cabeças grandes. No entanto, eles tinham corpos pequenos. Daniel reuniu coragem. Ele foi em direção a eles para perguntar por que eles vieram para a Terra. Um deles disse que ele veio coletar algumas amostras da Terra. Eles andaram no parque por algum tempo. Eles pegaram algumas rochas, flores e pedras. Era hora deles partirem. Daniel ficou completamente chocado. Os alienígenas entraram no disco voador e voaram para longe.
