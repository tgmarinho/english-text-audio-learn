## English

Albert came home from the office. He was very tired. He had his dinner. He went to his bed. He was trying to sleep. His friend called him. He didn’t want to talk because he was tired. He switched off his phone. He tried to sleep again. He again got a call on another phone. He was very angry. He didn’t answer it. He sat down and had some water. Someone knocked on his door. He opened the door. His friends came with cakes and gifts. It was Albert’s birthday. He forgot his birthday. He was very happy to see his friends. He said thank you. They had a party. Then Albert went to sleep.

## Português

Albert voltou do escritório. Estava muito cansado. Ele jantou. Ele foi para a cama. Ele estava tentando dormir. O amigo dele ligou para ele. Ele não queria falar porque estava cansado. Ele desligou o telefone. Ele tentou dormir novamente. Ele recebeu novamente uma ligação no outro telefone. Ele estava muito zangado. Ele não atendeu. Ele se sentou e tomou um pouco de água. Alguém bateu na porta. Ele abriu a porta. Seus amigos vieram com bolos e presentes. Era o aniversário de Albert. Ele esqueceu seu aniversário. Ele ficou muito feliz em ver seus amigos. Ele disse obrigado. Eles fizeram uma festa. Então Albert foi dormir.
