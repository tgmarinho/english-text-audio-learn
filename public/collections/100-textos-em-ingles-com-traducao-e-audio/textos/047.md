Ralph is a running champion. He has participated in many championships. He always wins medals. He has made his country proud. He has many fans. He has become a celebrity. He is a very good person. He shares his techniques with his juniors. He was congratulated by the government for his wonderful performance. His friends call him “Ralph the runner”. He is very fit. He runs very fast. He practices daily. He goes to the gym daily. He eats only healthy food. His parents are proud of him. He loves traveling. He wants to go on a world tour. He keeps learning new techniques.
