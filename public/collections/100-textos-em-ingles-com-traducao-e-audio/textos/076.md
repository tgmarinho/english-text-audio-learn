## English

Simon and Rihanna were good friends. They shared a great bond. They worked in the same office. Simon was rude. He engaged himself in an ugly fight with his colleague. Rihanna was very upset with Simon. She did not support his behavior. Rihanna tried to calm down Simon. Simon’s colleague decided to complain about him to his boss. Rihanna was very scared. She knew that the boss would fire Simon. Rihanna tried speaking to Simon’s colleague, but he was not ready to meet them halfway. Simon was in trouble. Simon’s colleague complained to his boss. Simon was scolded badly. He had to leave his office. He learned a lesson. He decided not to be rude to anyone.
