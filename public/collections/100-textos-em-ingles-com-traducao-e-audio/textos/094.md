## English

Jennifer wanted to buy a washing machine. She had saved some money. The washing machine would have made her work easier. It is easy to wash and dry clothes. It saves time. It is faster. It does not require physical strength. In winters it is a great help. Clothes can be dried in the machine. There is no sunlight outside during winter. Jennifer was not very rich. She could not afford to buy a washing machine. She asked her husband for it. He did not agree. Jennifer saved from her salary. She had saved enough. She could now buy it. She was happy to bring it home. She chose a company. She bought a washing machine of her favorite color.
