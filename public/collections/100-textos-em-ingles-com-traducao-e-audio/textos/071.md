## English

It was Emily’s birthday. She was very happy. Her mother bought a beautiful pink dress for her. She looked pretty in it. Emily’s parents arranged a party for her in the evening. Emily’s friends came home. The party began. Emily’s father was still at the office. She was waiting for him. Emily was upset. She did not cut the cake. She called her father. He said that he would be late. He had some work in the office. Emily was very angry with her father. Her father came home after the party was over. He brought a big doll for her. Emily was not talking to him. He showed her the doll and asked,” Do you want it or not?” Emily jumped with happiness. She loved the doll. She forgave her father.

## Português

Era aniversário de Emily. Ela estava muito feliz. Sua mãe comprou um lindo vestido rosa para ela. Ela estava bonita nele. Os pais de Emily organizaram uma festa para ela à noite. Os amigos de Emily chegaram em casa. A festa começou. O pai de Emily ainda estava no escritório. Ela estava esperando por ele. Emily estava chateada. Ela não cortou o bolo. Ela ligou para o pai. Ele disse que chegaria atrasado. Ele tinha trabalho no escritório. Emily estava muito zangada com o pai. Seu pai voltou para casa depois que a festa terminou. Ele trouxe uma boneca grande para ela. Emily não estava falando com ele. Ele mostrou a boneca e perguntou: “Você quer ou não?” Emily pulou de felicidade, amou a boneca e perdoou seu pai.
