## English

Ella was a college student. She was very weak. Doctors came to her school. They came to give flu shots to students. She did not want to get the flu shot. She was very scared. It would hurt her a lot. She started to cry. She hid in her classroom. She had taken a flu shot last year. It hurt her a lot. She fell sick. She was admitted to a hospital. It had affected her badly. She decided not to take the flu shot ever again. She asked her teacher to call her mother. Her mother came to school. She spoke to the doctor. She told the doctor, “No flu shot for Ella.” Ella was very happy. She was relaxed.

## Português

Ella era uma estudante universitária. Estava muito fraca. Os médicos vieram para a escola dela. Eles vieram dar vacinas contra a gripe aos estudantes. Ela não queria tomar a vacina contra a gripe. Ela estava muito assustada. Isso iria machucá-la muito. Ela começou a chorar. Ela se escondeu em sua sala de aula. Ela tinha tomado uma vacina contra a gripe no ano passado. Isso doía muito nela. Ela adoeceu. Ela foi internada em um hospital. Isso a afetara muito. Ela decidiu não tomar a vacina contra a gripe nunca mais. Ela pediu ao professor para ligar para a mãe. A mãe dela veio para a escola. Ela falou com o médico. Ela disse ao médico: “não vacine a Ela contra a gripe.” Ella estava muito feliz. Estava relaxada.
