## English

Mathew was a good boy. He went to school daily. He wore a clean uniform. He completed all his homework and projects. He scored good grades. His parents loved him. But he had no friends. He was very thin, so they made fun of him. Mathew was very upset. Nobody talked to him. He ate his lunch alone. One day, his teacher saw him sitting alone. He asked Matthew about the problem. Mathew told him that he had no friends. His teacher called all the students. The teacher said that Mathew is a good boy. He is special. He gets the best grades. He makes the best project. The teacher asked the students to say sorry to Matthew. Everyone said sorry to Matthew. He now had many friends. Mathew was very happy.

## Português

Mathew era um bom menino. Ele ia para a escola diariamente. Ele usava um uniforme limpo. Ele fazia todos os seus trabalhos de casa e projetos. Ele tirava boas notas. Seus pais o amavam. Mas ele não tinha amigos. Ele era muito magro, então eles zombaram dele. Mathew estava muito chateado. Ninguém falava com ele. Ele almoçava sozinho. Um dia, seu professor o viu sentado sozinho. Ele perguntou a Matthew qual era o problema. Mathew disse que ele não tinha amigos. Seu professor chamou todos os alunos. O professor disse que Mathew é um bom menino. Ele é especial. Ele tira as melhores notas. Ele faz o melhor projeto. O professor pediu aos alunos para pedir desculpas a Matthew. Todo mundo pediu desculpas a Matthew. Ele agora tinha muitos amigos. Mathew estava muito feliz.
