## English

Robert took a day off from work. He was very bored. He wanted to relax. He wanted to have some fun. He wanted to relive his childhood days. He was tired of working daily. He called his best friend, Peter. He asked Peter to come to his place. Peter came to his place. They had lunch together. They had coffee. Robert told Peter, “Let’s go fishing.” Peter agreed. He was very happy. They went fishing when they were in school. It was their hobby. Robert and Peter were childhood friends. Robert quickly got ready. They went to the nearby pond. They caught many fish. They were very happy. Robert felt very relaxed. He was happy to go to work again.

Robert tirou um dia de folga do trabalho. Estava muito entediado. Ele queria relaxar. Ele queria se divertir. Ele queria reviver seus dias de infância. Ele estava cansado de trabalhar diariamente. Ele chamou seu melhor amigo, Peter. Ele pediu a Peter para vir para sua casa. Pedro veio a sua casa. Eles almoçaram juntos. Eles tomaram café. Robert disse a Peter: “Vamos pescar.” Peter concordou. Ele estava muito feliz. Eles iam pescar quando estavam na escola. Era seu hobby. Robert e Peter eram amigos de infância. Robert rapidamente se aprontou. Eles foram para a lagoa próxima. Pegaram muitos peixes. Eles estavam muito felizes. Robert sentiu-se muito relaxado. Ele estava feliz por voltar a trabalhar.
