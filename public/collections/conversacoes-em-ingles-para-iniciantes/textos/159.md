## English

A: Alex, I bought my first car.

B: Wow! It’s fantastic.

A: Yeah, It’s very fast.

B: I think it cost a fortune.

A: Yeah, It cost U$ 500,000.00

B: ohhh my goshhh!!! A half a billion dollars…

A: The girls will want me now.

B: Why are you telling me that?

A: Girls like cars.

B: No, that’s not true.
