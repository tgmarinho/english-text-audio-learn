## English

B: Cuiaba is really hot!

A: Yes, it is.

B: I’m very thirsty.

A: Let’s go drink a juice.

B: Sure, where?

A: Perpitolla Juice.

B: Let’s go there.

A: Right, should we call Anne?

B: Yeah, call her.
