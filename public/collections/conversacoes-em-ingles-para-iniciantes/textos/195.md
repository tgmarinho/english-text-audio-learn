## English

A: What are you doing?

B: I’m washing our car.

A: Why are you washing it?

B: I’m washing It because it’s dirty.

A: would you like some help?

B: No, I can do it by myself. Thanks.

A: No problem.

B: I’d like to drive this car someday.

A: When you grow up you will drive as many cars as you want.

B: I want to grow up soon.
