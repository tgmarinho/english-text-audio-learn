## English

A: I’m so sick.

B: What’s the matter?

A: My head hurts. I have a headache.

B: Did you take medicine?

A: yeah, I took medicine. But my head still hurts.

B: You should go to the doctor.

A: Yeah, I have to make an appointment.

B: I have the number.

A: Give it to me, please.

B: 444-963-551

B: Você deve ir ao médico.
