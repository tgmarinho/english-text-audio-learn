## English

A: Hey, Frank. I’m going to Rio next month.

B: That’s nice.

A: would you like to come with me?

B: What are we going to do in Rio?

A: Well, we can visit many beaches, landscapes and so on.

B: I don’t have enough money

A: oh, really? But It’s not expensive to go there.

B: How much does it cost?

A: It will be about £ 5.000,00

B: Nice, I will think about it.
