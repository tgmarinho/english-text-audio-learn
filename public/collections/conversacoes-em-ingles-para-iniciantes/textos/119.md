## English

A: Hello Mathilda, how are you?

B: Fine, and you?

A: Fine too, thanks!

B: What’s the News.

A: I bought a new apartment!

B: oh, that’s great!

A: Where did you buy it?

B: I bought it in San Francisco.

A: Great! Now I have a place to visit in summer.

B: It’ll be a pleasure

## Português

A: Onde você comprou?

B: Comprei em San Francisco.
