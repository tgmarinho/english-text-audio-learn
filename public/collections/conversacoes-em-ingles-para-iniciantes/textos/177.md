## English

A: Hey. I’d like to see these jeans.

B: That’ one on the top of the shelf?

A: yeah, That’s it.

B: Here you are.

A: wow! It’s beautiful.

B: yeah, It is. We only have this one.

A: How much does it cost?

B: It costs R$ 60,00

A: Okay. I’ll buy it.

B: Will you pay with a Credit Card?
