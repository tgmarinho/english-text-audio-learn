## English

A: Hi, John! Are you still learning English:

B: Yes, I am. I’m studying English Every day.

A: Cool! have you ever heard about AIG?

B: No, what is it?

A: AIG is a website where you can learn English for free.

B: Oh, that’s really great! How does it work?

A: It’s easy! You only have to access their website.

B: Please, give me the website address

A: Yes, I’ll give it to you. It’s a great platform. Everyone likes it.

B: It’s sounds good. I’ll access it.
