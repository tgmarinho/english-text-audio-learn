## English

A: Hey Margarida, how are you?

B: I’m great, Thank you. What’s up?

A: Just working.

B: That’s great! What will you buy today?

A: I want a Subway ticket.

B: Anything else?

A: No, just that. How much?

B: £ 20,00

A: I’ll pay with cash.

B: alright.
