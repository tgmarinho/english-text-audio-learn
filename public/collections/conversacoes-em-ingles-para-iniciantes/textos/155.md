## English

A: Hello, Elizabeth.

B: Hello Pietra.

A: is that house still available?

B: yes, it is.

A: I have a friend who wants to buy a house.

B: Great,

A: I passed your contact to him

B: Excellent, I will wait for his call

A: See you later!

B: Bye- bye.

## Português
