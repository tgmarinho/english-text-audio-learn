## English

A: Did you see the new engineer?

B: yes, I saw him. He is very smart.

A: He told me he used to work for “Trump’s Building Company”

B: Really? Cool. So he is a great engineer.

A: yeah, and he speaks 4 languages.

B: A polyglot? Ohh, this is why he was hired. He is very clever.

A: Peter, how many languages do you speak?

B: Two languages.

A: Which language?

B: Mandarin and English.
