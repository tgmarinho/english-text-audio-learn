## English

A: Do you like nature?

B: I love it.

A: I usually go the mountains.

A: Really?

B: The waterfalls are amazing.

A: Nature is amazing.

B: Do you want to go to the mountains with me?

A: I would love.

B: we will leave tomorrow at 7 am.
