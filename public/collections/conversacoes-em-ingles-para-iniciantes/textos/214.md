## English

A: Hello!

B: Hi, Can I help you?

A: Sure, I’d like to go to San Francisco. And I need to rent a car.

B: Sure, What’s your full name?

A: Johnson Smith.

B: What’s your address?

A: Ritch Main Street, 41, Florida

B: How many days?

A: 2 weeks

B: $ 2.000 Dolars.
