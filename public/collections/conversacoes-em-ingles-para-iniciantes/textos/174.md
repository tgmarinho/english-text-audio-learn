## English

A: hey, Patricia! Who is that guy?

B: I don’t know him. He is new here.

A: He is so handsome.

B: Yeah. He has green eyes.

A: Why don’t you go there and talk to him?

B: I don’t know. Maybe he has a girlfriend.

A: Go there just to talk.

B: Yeah, great idea. I will go over.

A: Good luck!

B: Thanks

## Português

B: Eu não sei. Talvez ele tenha uma namorada.
