## English

A: Amanda, did you change your computer?

B: Yes, I did.

A: It is very nice!.

B: The other computer was too small.

A: What did you do with it?

B: It’s at home, I’m selling it.

A: What is the price?

B: $ 500.

A: I want to buy it

B : I’ll bring it tomorrow.
