## English

B: Yeah, It’s very difficult

A: Are you getting good grades?

B: Yes, I study hard.

A: You are a good student.

B: The subject is difficult but I need to study it.

A: Studying is very important.

B: I want to go to college after school.

A: Nice dream.

B: Thank you.
