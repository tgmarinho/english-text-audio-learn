## English

A: I watched a scary movie.

B: I don’t like that kind.

A: I prefer comedy.

B: Do you want to watch a comedy movie?

A: When?

B: On Saturday

A: Of course.

B: I will send you my address

A: I can’t wait.

B: I will see you there.

## Português

A: Quando?

B: No sábado
