## English

A: Josh, My motorcycle is broken.

B: What happened?

A: I don’t know, It doesn’t want to start.

B: have you checked the oil?

A: Yes, I have. It’s okay.

B: Have you checked the battery?

A: The battery? No, I haven’t.

B: It would be good idea if you check the battery.

A: Ohh, probably the Battery ran out.

B: Yes, that may be the problem.

## Português

B: Seria uma boa ideia se você verificar a bateria.
