## English

A: This city never sleeps.

B: Yeah, I have lived in NYC for about 30 years and I haven’t seen it sleep.

A: Why do people really like New York?

B: I don’t know, maybe because of these incredible buildings?

A: Maybe because of the lights?

B: yeah, or maybe because of Central Park?

A: Millions and millions of people come here every day.

B: This proves they really love NYC.

A: Of course, “If you can make it here, you can make it anywhere”

B: New York, New York
