## English

A: Anything else?

B: No, just that, please!

A: $ 3.000 dollars.

B: I’ll pay by cheque

A: Okay. Sign here please.

B: Done!

A: Thanks for buying here.

B: You’re welcome.

A: Every week our products are on sale.

B: It’s nice to know that.
