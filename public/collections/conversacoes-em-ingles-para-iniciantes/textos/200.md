## English

A: Hello.

B: Hi.

A: Do you speak English?

B: Yes, but I speak only a little.

A: Nice. I’m looking for Guarujá Beach.

B: Are you American?

A: Yes, I am. It’s my first time here in Brazil

B: Cool! Welcome to my country.

A: Thanks

B: No problem.
