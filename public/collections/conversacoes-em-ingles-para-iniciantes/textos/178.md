## English

A: I need to go to New York.

B: One-way ticket or return ticket?

A: A return, please.

B: U$ 1,000.00

A: Okay, I’ll pay with cash.

B: okay. The airplane departs at 8:00 o’clock

A: Thank you.

B: is there a restaurant nearby here?

A: yeah, downstairs.

B: okay. Thanks.
