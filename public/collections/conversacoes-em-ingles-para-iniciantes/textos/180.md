## English

A: George, I’m trying to learn guitar. Can you help me?

B: Oh, that’s nice! For sure, why not?

A: When can we start?

B: We can try at the weekend.

A: Oh, that’s very great!

B: You will learn quickly!

A: What songs will we practice.

B: We will start playing the Beatles.

A: I love “Hey Jude”.

B: This song is very easy.
