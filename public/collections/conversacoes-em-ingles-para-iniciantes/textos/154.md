## English

A: Hello!

B: Hello! Do you sell t-shirts?

A: which color t-shirt would you like?

B: I like black t-shirts.

A: is this t-shirt too big?

B: yes, too big.

A: Do you have a smaller t-shirt?

B: Yes, I have a smaller t-shirt.

A: Does it fit?

B: Yes, it fits.
