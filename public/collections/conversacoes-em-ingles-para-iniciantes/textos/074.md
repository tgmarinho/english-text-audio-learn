## English

A: I went to do the exam today.

B: Hey, how did it go?

A: my pen stopped during the exam.

B: Did you get another pen?

A: I had to borrow one from my colleague.

B: Thank God you did that!

A: Yes, I was very happy.

B: did you do well on the test?

A: I got a good grade.

B: Congratulations!
