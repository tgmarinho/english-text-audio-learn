## English

A: Hi! I’ve talked to you earlier.

B: yeah, I remember!

A: I forgot to ask you.

B: alright.

A: Where are you from?

B: I’m from England

A: That’s nice! My parents live there.

B: Cool! Where They live?

A: They live on Liverpool

B: That’s good!
