## English

A: Hello, Alice Wayne

B: Hello, Mr. Dimitri.

A: Do you want to go on a student exchange program to Russia?

B: Yes, I want to live abroad.

A: You are a good student!

B: I study every day.

A: You have good qualities for the exchange.

B: Thank you!

A: You will love Russia.

B: It seems to be a great place.
