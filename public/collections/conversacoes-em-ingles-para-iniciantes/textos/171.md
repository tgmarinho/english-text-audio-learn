## English

A: Hey! Today I’m going to the party.

B: What party?

A: Nicolle’s Party!

B: That sounds good!

A: Would you like to go with me?

B: Not really. Today, I’m very busy.

A: Hey! Today is Saturday.

B: yes, I know, But I’m doing a lot of things.

A: okay, then. See you later.

B: bye bye
