## English

A: Good morning, Philip.

B: Good morning, Mark.

A: How good are the vegetables?

B: good, do you want some?

A: I want a lettuce.

B: They are succulent.

A: I’m going to make a salad today.

B: Do you want tomatoes?

A: Oh yes, please!

B: Your salad will look great
