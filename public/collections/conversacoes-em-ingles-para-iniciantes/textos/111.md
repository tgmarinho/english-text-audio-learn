## English

A: Billy, can you lend me your black jacket?

B: For what?

A: I am going to the mountains at the weekend.

B: This is my favorite jacket.

A: I will use it only once.

B: I don’t know …

A: I’ll take care of it!

B: do you promise?

A: Of course.

B: All right then.
