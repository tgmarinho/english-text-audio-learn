## English

A: Hey Rachel, what do you think?

B: Think of what?

A: What you think about my camera?

B: It’s nice!

A: I bought it yesterday.

B: How much does it cost?

A: It costs U$ 100,00.

B: Where did you buy it?

A: I bought it at “Best Buy”

B: It’s really cheap!
