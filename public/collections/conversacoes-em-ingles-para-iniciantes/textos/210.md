## English

A: Mark did you know about Frank?

B: No, what happened?

A: he died last night?

B: Oh, Gosh! Really? I don’t believe it!

A: Yeah, that’s so terrible

B: My condolences. But how did you know that?

A: I watched on TV.

B: That’s very bad news.

A: His family is so sad.

B: He was a nice guy.
