## English

Excuse me, what’s your name?

My name is Jessica. What’s yours?

John.

You speak English very well.

Thank you.

Do you know what time it is?

Sure. It’s 5:10PM.

What did you say?

I said it’s 5:10PM.

Thanks.

You’re welcome.

Jessica:O que você disse?
