## English

Mr. and Mrs. Smith are retired.Mr. Smith is seventy-one years old and Mrs. Smith is sixty-six.They have two small children and three grandchildren.Mr. and Mrs. Smith love to walk in the park.Their grandchildren Tom, Jenny and Rose love their grandparents very much.Today they are walking in the park together.Mr. and Mrs. Smith sit on the bench and their grandchildren play next to them.Tom and Jenny play hide and seek.And little Rose plays in the sand-pit. She makes sand cupcakes.It is a lovely sunny day today.There are a lot of people and kids in the park.Tomorrow Mr. and Mrs. Smith will come here again.
