## English

This is a boy.He is eleven years old.He is tall and slim.He has curly brown hair and big green eyes.His name is James.He lives in a small house in the center of a big city.James lives with his parents.His parents work all day long.James go to school but has no friends.He feels lonely.James asks his parents to get a dog or a cat.But his parents don’t allow James do this.He comes back from school and hears some noise.He hears a dog howling.James looks around and sees a big dog at the gas station.The dog is very beautiful but skinny.James has a sandwich in his bag.It is a tasty ham sandwich.He gives it to the dog.The dog eats it and licks James on the face.Both the boy and the dog are happy.James has a friend now.
