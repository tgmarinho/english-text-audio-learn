## English

This is Alice.She is a schoolgirl.She has a lot of friends at school.Soon there is a talent show at her school.Participants will dance, sing, act and play musical instruments.Alice doesn’t know what to choose.She sings very well.Her Mom says that Alice should be a singer.Also Alice plays the piano and the violin.Alice attends dancing classes after school.So she can prepare a dance for the contest.Her sister acts in an amateur theater and can give her a pair of lessons.Alice can act a mini-play.So what to choose?She’s good at a lot of things.

## Português
