## English

Hello! My name is Jeremy Thompson.I live in Newcastle, England.I am thirteen years old.I am a schoolboy.I love playing sports with my friends.My favorite sport is soccer.My favorite soccer player is Wayne Rooney.I play soccer with my friends every day after school.But also I love reading.I love different books: adventures, fantasy and sci-fi.My favorite book is The Lord of the Rings by J.R.R. Tolkien.It is a very interesting book.I love reading about adventures of Frodo Baggins.
