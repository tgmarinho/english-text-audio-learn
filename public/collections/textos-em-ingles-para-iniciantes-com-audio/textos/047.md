## English

I have a friend.Her name is Jessica.Her father has his own business.Her mother doesn’t work.Jessica is only sixteen but she has a car.Her father bought it for her last month.Jessica goes to school by her car.She also buys new clothes every weekend.She doesn’t count money.Jessica never knows how much she spends.But all the time she says that she needs more money.She always wants more than she already has.
