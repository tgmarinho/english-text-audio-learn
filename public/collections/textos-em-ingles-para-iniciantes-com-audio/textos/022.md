## English

Once upon a time there lived a cat and a mouse.Usually cats hunt and catch mice but they were good friends.They lived in a big rich house and even had their own servants.The cat was white and fluffy so he had a personal servant to comb his hair.The mouse was so little that he had a personal servant to carry him in a basket.The cat and the mouse loved to spend time together.They had so many ideas about what to do: to play hide and seek in the house, to sing their favorite songs or just to sit in the porch and watch the sun go down.Once they were sitting in the porch and discussing their new day.The cat suggested a new game – a race.The mouse was astonished because the cat was much bigger than the mouse, so he could easily win the race.But the cat laughed and said that they would make their servants take part in the race.The mouse loved the idea and they shook their paws in agreement.

## Português

Era uma vez um gato e um rato.Geralmente os gatos caçam e pegam ratos mas eles eram bons amigos.Eles moravam em uma grande casa rica e até tinham seus próprios servos.O gato era branco e fofo ele tinha um empregado pessoal para pentear seu cabelo.O rato era tão pequeno que ele tinha um criado pessoal para carregá-lo em uma cesta.O gato e o rato gostavam de passar tempo juntos.Eles tinham tantas ideias sobre o que fazer: brincar de esconde-esconde na casa, cantar suas canções favoritas ou apenas sentar-se na varanda e ver o sol se pôr.Uma vez que eles estavam sentados na varanda e discutindo seu novo dia.O gato sugeriu um novo jogo – uma corrida.O rato ficou espantado porque o gato era muito maior que o rato, então ele poderia facilmente ganhar a corrida.Mas o gato riu e disse que fariam seus criados tomar parte na corrida.O rato amou a ideia e eles apertaram as patas de acordo.
