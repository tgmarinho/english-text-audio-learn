## English

Jenna is in high school.She wants to buy a present for her Mom’s birthday.She needs to earn some money in her summer holidays.She looks through job offers on the internet.There are a lot of offers in different areas.All the vacancies have a range of requirements and skills.Jenna is a schoolgirl and has no special education.But she wants to buy a great present for her Mom.So she keeps on looking.Finally she finds a vacancy for a secretary in a small firm.She reads the notice very attentively.There are no special skills to do this job.So she looks through the things a secretary does.A secretary answers phone calls, plans the boss’s day, helps with papers, meets clients in the office.Jenna can do this easily.So she picks up the phone and calls.
