## English

This is Andrew Rampling.He is an engineer.He is forty-seven.And he is single.He lives with his elderly mother.Andrew works for a small construction firm.He projects commercial buildings.Andrew is a kind and friendly person.He is always polite with his colleagues.The colleagues are polite too.But they mock him behind his back.They call him Mr. Jacket.Andrew’s mother knits jackets for him.And he wears his jackets in the office.He has a lot of jackets now.And he changes them every day.Andrew loves his mother very much.
