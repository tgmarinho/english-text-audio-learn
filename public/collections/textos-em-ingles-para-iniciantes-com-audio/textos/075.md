## English

Things change and we must change with them.We must get used to living at high speed.Day after day we change the world around us.We discover new lands, we build huge houses, we reach for the skies.Our houses are full of labor-saving gadgets.Just look inside your kitchen to see what I mean!A mixer, a blender, a microwave oven, a frost-free-fridge.
