## English

This day started with a wonderful morning.I woke up at eight o’clock, opened my eyes and smiled.The sun was shining so brightly that all my room was full of light.I took a shower and started looking for a special dress.Why special? Because it was a special day – my sister was getting married.I came across all the dresses I had in my wardrobe and finally found a beautiful one.It was a long bright blue dress with small white flowers on it.Then I went down the stairs and found my parents and my sister in the living room.

## Português

Este dia começou com uma manhã maravilhosa.Acordei às oito horas, abri os olhos e sorri.O sol estava brilhando tão forte que todo o meu quarto estava cheio de luz.Tomei banho e comecei a procurar um vestido especial.Por que especial? Porque era um dia especial – minha irmã ia se casar.Me deparei todos os vestidos que tinha no guarda-roupa e finalmente encontrei um lindo.Era um vestido longo azul brilhante com pequenas flores brancas nele.Então desci as escadas e encontrei meus pais e minha irmã na sala de estar.
