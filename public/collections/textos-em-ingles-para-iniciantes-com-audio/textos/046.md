## English

Sam has finished his studies at university.He has had a farewell party with his friends.Now he wants a job in the gaming industry.He wants to work abroad.He has already looked through the vacancies.He has chosen several positions in Canada and France.He fits all the requirements: he speaks French fluently, he has an education in web programming, he has a portfolio with his own project developing.The only thing he needs is practice.His other problem is to get prepared for a job interview.He has to do his best and show his vivid interest in that position.So Sam has been training at the mirror the whole week.Tomorrow he’s gonna do his first job interview.He hopes he will look respectable and smart.
