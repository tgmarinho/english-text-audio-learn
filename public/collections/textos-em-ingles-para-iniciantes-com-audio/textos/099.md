## English

Today I was sorting through old pictures.I noticed a photo with a girl with an extremely short haircut.She seemed familiar.But I couldn’t remember who she was.Maybe she was our relative.I called my Mom.She came in and saw the photo.I asked her about the girl.She smiled and said that she was that girl.She was seventeen in that picture.She loved punk rock music.And she had that haircut and dyed her hair green.Her parents were shocked.Perhaps I should try it too?

## Português

Hoje eu estava separando imagens antigas.Eu notei uma foto com uma menina com um corte de cabelo extremamente curto.Ela parecia familiar.Mas eu não conseguia me lembrar de quem ela era.Talvez ela fosse nossa parente.Liguei para minha mãe.Ela veio e viu a foto.Perguntei-lhe sobre a menina.Ela sorriu e disse que era aquela garota.Ela tinha dezessete anos naquela foto.Adorava a música punk rock.E ela tinha aquele corte de cabelo e tingiu seu cabelo verde.Seus pais ficaram chocados.Talvez eu deva tentar isso também?
