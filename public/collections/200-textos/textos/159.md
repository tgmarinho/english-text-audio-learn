## English

Dakota is shopping at the mall. It’s a typical Tuesday afternoon. She is in her favorite store and she is looking for a new purse. Suddenly, she hears some gun shots. Everyone around starts panicking. There’s a man with a gun in the shopping center. She tries to run away, but the workers at the store she is currently in suggest that they should all stay inside the store. They close the doors and hide themselves. It was the best decision to make. They possibly saved themselves. As soon as the police arrive, everyone is able to get out of the mall. Fortunately, no one was hurt and the man got arrested. It was a scary day for Dakota.
