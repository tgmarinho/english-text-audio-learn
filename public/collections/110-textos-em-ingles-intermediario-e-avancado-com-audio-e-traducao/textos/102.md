## English

When Martine has to go to work, she has to walk by a park. Every Wednesday morning, there are always a lot of people at the park. She didn’t know why but she was curious. One day, she decided to stop by and to ask what was happening. Her neighbor Tony told her that there was tango every Wednesday morning at the park. It was free classes for whoever wanted to try. At first, Martine wasn’t interested, but then, Tony convinced her to try it with him. After all, it was free. She has to go to work an hour later, but it’s totally worth it. Tango at the park is actually very nice. Martine is enjoying those classes and after a couple of weeks, she is getting really good. When the summer ended, tango classes at the park stopped. Tony and Martine were sad, but they had a good idea. They decided to take private tango classes every Wednesday night during wintertime.

## Português

Quando Martine tem que ir trabalhar, ela tem que andar por um parque. Todas as quartas-feiras de manhã, tem sempre um monte de gente no parque. Ela não sabia por quê, mas estava curiosa. Um dia, ela decidiu parar e perguntar o que estava acontecendo. Seu vizinho Tony lhe disse que havia tango todas as quartas-feiras de manhã no parque. Eram aulas gratuitas para quem quisesse tentar. No início, Martine não estava interessada, mas então, Tony a convenceu a tentar (fazer as aulas) com ele. Afinal, era grátis. Ela tem que ir trabalhar uma hora depois, mas vale a pena. Tango no parque é realmente muito bom. Martine está curtindo essas aulas e depois de algumas semanas, ela está ficando muito boa. Quando o verão acabou, as aulas de tango no parque pararam. Tony e Martine ficaram tristes, mas tiveram uma boa ideia. Eles decidiram fazer aulas particulares de tango todas as noites de quarta-feira durante o inverno.
