## English

It was late at night and it was raining outside. Richard was coming back from the movie theater by himself, in his car. He was trying to stay focused on the road, but his eyes were closing by themselves. He was very tired. Suddenly, he hit something. Horrified, he stopped his car on the side of the road and got out. He tried to see if there was an animal. It turned out to be a Pigeon. The pigeon wasn’t dead, but he was suffering. Richard felt guilty, the poor animal was fighting for his life. He had the good idea to call a veterinarian that he knows. He knew he was offering 24 hours services. The veterinarian told him to take the pigeon with him to his office. Richard took the pigeon and drove with him to the veterinarian’s office. It took a couple of hours, but he was able to save him. Richard couldn’t believe he did such a thing, but he is happy to know he saved a pigeon that night.

## Português

Era tarde da noite e chovia lá fora. Richard estava voltando do cinema sozinho, em seu carro. Ele estava tentando manter o foco na estrada, mas seus olhos estavam se fechando sozinhos. Ele estava muito cansado. De repente, ele bateu em alguma coisa. Horrorizado, ele parou o carro na beira da estrada e saiu. Tentou ver se havia um animal. Acabou sendo um pombo. O pombo não estava morto, mas estava sofrendo. Richard se sentiu culpado, o pobre animal estava lutando por sua vida. Ele teve a boa ideia de chamar um veterinário que ele conhece. Ele sabia que estava oferecendo 24 horas de serviços. O veterinário disse-lhe para levar o pombo com ele para o seu consultório. Richard pegou o pombo e o levou para o consultório do veterinário. Demorou algumas horas, mas ele conseguiu salvá-lo. Richard não podia acreditar que fez uma coisa dessas, mas está feliz em saber que salvou um pombo naquela noite.
